DSC180B Capstone: Face Mask Detector with Explainable Artificial Intelligence

Introduction

Face Mask Detector

Explainable AI is a useful algorithm that increases the transparency in machine learning models for evaluation purposes. In our section, we specifically learned about an explainable AI model called Grad-CAM, which is often used to validate the accuracy of image classifiers. We learned about the usefulness of the Grad-CAM algorithm through the replication project, and it inspired us to propose these following ideas for the capstone project. We would like to build a face mask detector using convolutional neural networks and validate its reliablibity using an explainable AI algorithm. We proposed this idea because we are currently in a global pandemic due to the spread of SARSCOVID-19. The government encourages people to wear masks in the public, and a face mask detector would help to enforce this policy by locating individuals without masks before they enter any facilities. We expect our face mask detector to detect if the person in the image is correctly wearing a mask by covering their nose, chin, and mouth. In addition, we plan to use an explainable AI algorithm to validate the face mask detector to ensure its accuracy.

Why is this Important

The dataset consists of 1376 images with people wearing masks or not. Adrian managed to successfully build a face mask detection tool and achieve tremendous results. However, the dataset only consisted of 1376 images, which is fairly small for a dataset used for computer vision tasks. In addition, the dataset only has images with people wearing masks or not. It doesn’t contain images where a person is wearing a mask but not covering his nose or chin. In other words, it doesn’t have images where a person is wearing a mask improperly. We believe that this is a deficiency that is important to address because it is very likely that a lot of people won’t be wearing their masks properly during this ongoing pandemic, and in a situation where this issue becomes serious, such as hosting a public event where everyone is required to wear a mask, it’s essential to incorporate a face mask detector that can not only detect a person is wearing a mask but also whether they are wearing it properly.
